Dhal curry

Home Made Sri Lankan Chicken Curry

Sri Lankan college students guide to easy Dhal (lentil) curry

Ingredients

  • Dhal
  • Onions
  • Tumeric powder
  • Curry powder
  • Chilli powder
  • garlic
  • Salt
  • Coconut milk
  • oil

Steps

  1. Heat up oil and add onions untill slightly brown.
  2. chilli powder ( to your preffered amount ). and some chillie pieces (optional)
  3. add half a tea spoon of curry podwer and half a tea spoon of tumeric podwer.
  4. Put in the (washed) Dhal and mix well and keep it for 2 minutes.
  5. add salt and mix well.
  6. add water till it covers the dhal and let it simmer till fully cooked.(it will turn yellow)
